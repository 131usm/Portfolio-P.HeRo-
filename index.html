Welcome To to the world of Winston Churchill, one of the greatest statesmen of the 20th century!

Churchill was a British politician, writer, and orator who served as the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1940 to 1945 and again from 1951 to 1955. Born on November 30, 1874, in Woodstock, England, Churchill was educated at Harrow School and the Royal Military College, Sandhurst. He served in the British Army and as a war correspondent before being elected to Parliament in 1900. Over the next several decades, Churchill held a number of important government positions, including Home Secretary, Chancellor of the Exchequer, and First Lord of the Admiralty.
